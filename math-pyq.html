2025 - Board Exam Questions

Q1. Which of the following is not a rational number?

A) √2

B) 1/3

C) 5

D) -7

Solution: The answer is A) √2. A rational number is one that can be written as a fraction of two integers. √2 is an irrational number because it cannot be expressed as a fraction.

Q2. Write the prime factorization of 72.

Solution: To find the prime factorization of 72, we divide it by the smallest prime number until we get 1:

72 ÷ 2 = 36

36 ÷ 2 = 18

18 ÷ 2 = 9

9 ÷ 3 = 3

3 ÷ 3 = 1

Thus, the prime factorization of 72 is 23 × 32.

2023 - Board Exam Questions

Q1. Which of the following is an irrational number?

A) √5

B) -5/2

C) 0.75

D) 4

Solution: The answer is A) √5. √5 is irrational because it cannot be expressed as a fraction of integers.

Q2. Find the HCF of 36 and 60.

Solution: To find the HCF of 36 and 60, we perform prime factorization:

36 = 22 × 32

60 = 22 × 3 × 5

The common factors are 22 × 3. So, HCF = 12.

2022 - Board Exam Questions

Q1. Prove that the sum of two irrational numbers can be rational or irrational.

Solution: Let us assume two irrational numbers a and b.

Case 1: If a = √2 and b = -√2, then a + b = √2 + (-√2) = 0, which is rational.

Case 2: If a = √2 and b = √3, then a + b = √2 + √3, which is irrational.

Thus, the sum of two irrational numbers can be either rational or irrational.
